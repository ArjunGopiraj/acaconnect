MATHEMATICAL EXPLANATION:
--------------------------
CF Score = Base Score + (Co-registration Boost × 0.1)

Example:
Event: SQL WAR
Base Score: 1.0 (perfect tag match)
Co-registrations: 6 times with other events
CF Boost: 6 × 0.1 = 0.6
Final CF Score: min(1.0 + 0.6×0.1, 1.0) = 1.0 (capped)
